# Level Relay

Link two levels together and switch between them from the pause menu.

Press link level on one level, open another level, then press it again. After that the pause menu gets a switch button so u can go between the 2 easily.

Auto SP Select can also pick the nearest startpos in ur sp copy when u die in the original level. So if u die at 20% on slaughterhouse and the nearest sp before where u died is at 18%, switching after that will put u on the 18% sp.

Online linked levels load when u switch to them if gd doesnt already have them loaded.

If ur wondering I mainly made this mod for chimpguy on yt because i saw him having to do a lot js to go between slh sp and js normal slh.
